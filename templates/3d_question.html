How To Use AR Evidence

How To Use AR Evidence This page uses augmented reality(AR) technology to let you examine evidence from a crime on your phone. On the evidence page, swipe to spin the object in 3D space, or pinch to zoom in/out. Try tapping the "View In AR" button to place the object in your room. This will open your phone's camera so you can place the 3D evidence on the floor, or a tabletop. While the evidence appears as semi-transparent on your screen, slowly move your camera around the room. Once the scan is complete, the object will appear in your environment at full scale.
