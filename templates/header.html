ABOUT

An Innovative True Crime News App

CrimeDoor is the first app of its kind. Our groundbreaking Augmented Reality technology accurately recreates crime scenes and allows users to experience and investigate them —just as detectives at the actual crime scenes did. Using CrimeDoor you can explore evidence, review thousands of case files, look into cold cases, and help unravel True Crime’s most notorious unsolved mysteries.

  • VIEW and share photos of real crime scenes.
  • LISTEN to True Crime podcasts.
  • WALK directly into crime scenes.
  • WATCH videos related to unsolved cases.
  • READ news articles about hundreds of cases.
  • HELP solve notorious cold cases.
